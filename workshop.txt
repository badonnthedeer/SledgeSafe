title=SledgeSafe
description=SledgeSafe is a small mod that allows the use of sledgehammers in multiplayer without endangering safehouses!

description=

description=[h1]Details[/h1]

description=[list]

description=[*]A player that isn't part of a safehouse won't be able to destroy it. They will, however, be able to destroy unowned buildings!

description=[*]A player that is an owner of a safehouse will always be able to destroy it using a sledgehammer.

description=[*]Members of a safehouse are allowed to destroy a safehouse given the sandbox option "Safehouse Members Can Sledge" is turned on!

description=[*]Admins of servers can destroy any part of any safehouse.

description=[/list]
